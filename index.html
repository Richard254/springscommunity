We are an International NGO based in Kenya that Intervenes in the Plights of widows and orphans and other Vulnerable groups of people with an aim of supporting them towards self reliance.
It is our mission to work with women(widows and adolescent girls in difficult circumstances) and orphaned children in the rural and urban areas to promote sustainable development to eradicate poverty and to improve quality of life to everyone involved and their families.
To provide a safe, caring and loving environment for children who are vulnerable, orphaned or abandoned.

What We Do

We believe that we can save more lives by providing:

Food

This helps counter poverty, hunger and at the same time, it can improve harmony, friendliness and trust.

Clean Water

Women and children no longer have to walk miles to fetch water each day and thus giving them more time to learn

Health Care

Health care can influence a child's physical and emotional health, growth, and development and his/her capacity to reach their full potential as adults.

Education

Education in early childhood paves the way for success in school and it not only prepares a child for reading and writing but also builds his/her confidence.

Residential Facilities

Having a roof over one's head promotes safety and peace of mind.

Social Care

This enables individuals to live normal lives and carry out tasks they otherwise wouldn't be able to.
